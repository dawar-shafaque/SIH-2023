Relief Work Done

Disaster response requires rapid mobilization, but the help must be of the right type. The ministry's efforts are fast, yet always in direct response to specific requests from the people of our country. We work swiftly coordinating with other organizations,public agencies and volunteers to ensure the most efficient use of resources. We vouch to extend a helping hand to each and every citizen by connecting them to the people who can help them.
